Frequently Asked Questions

How to apply for government schemes?

You can apply for individual schemes directly through their respective official website portal using the "Visit Website" button provided in each scheme file card, or visit your nearest Common Services Center (CSC).

How to check eligibility criteria?

Every scheme listed on our portal highlights basic eligibility rules. Detailed qualifications can be validated on the scheme's formal webpage before initiating registration.

What documents are generally required?

Common mandatory documents include an identity proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport), Proof of Address, Income Certification, Passport size photographs, and relevant educational/category documents based on the specific scheme requirements.
